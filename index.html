About Our Alumni

Our Legacy Continues

We are the proud 2013 Year Group of NSTS — a family united by friendship, shared memories, and purpose. Our group was formed to strengthen the bond we built during our school days and to extend a helping hand to one another in times of need.
We believe in supporting each other, celebrating our successes, and standing together through challenges.

Beyond assistance, we aim to give back to our school and community, keeping the NSTS spirit alive wherever we go.


Together, we grow. Together, we make a difference
